About Blind Football India

Blind Football India is dedicated to promoting football among visually impaired individuals across the nation. We believe in inclusion, opportunity, and excellence for all athletes. Our mission is to empower blind and partially sighted footballers to achieve their dreams and compete at the highest levels of the sport.
